Produce a concise doctor report for hospital processing. Include:

1. Chief complaint and primary symptom
2. Duration, location, severity (1-10), additional symptoms
3. Health profile summary (past surgeries, chronic conditions, medications, allergies)
4. Specialist assessment (if any): which expert, key findings, whether photo was reviewed
5. Vitals (if available)
6. Triage: urgency, recommended department, reason

Format as clear, scannable sections. No treatment advice. For use by triage/nursing only.

Input data will be provided in structured form; output a short narrative or bullet summary.
